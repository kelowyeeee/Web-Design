Hello! Welcome to my portfolio!

I am Jiayue Zhang but everyone calls me Chloe! It is exciting to be able to create a portfolio website for all of my artworks that I have done! I am a student in University at Buffalo. I am currently a sophomore Studio Art major in Graphic Design concentration. I have been creating art in this university for 2 years now and I hope that in my following years, I get to create even more and being able to showcase it. I love to admire and create art. My favorite artist is Claude Monet from the Impressionism era. He is a great and inspiring artist. I hope in my career path as a Graphic Designer, I'll be influenced to use some of his elements in my own work! This portfolio contains all the artwork in different artforms, I have created. Many including paintings, sketches, digital art, and photography. I hope this portfolio is enjoyable.
